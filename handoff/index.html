03 / UPDATING INVENTORY

Add a car in about 90 seconds.

Every car on the website lives in a single editable list. To add, edit, or remove a car, you log into your admin page — no code, no GitHub, just a form.

Here’s the flow:

Go to pablitosautosales.com/admin/ → log in with your email → click Inventory → click Add Car → fill in year, make, model, price, miles, upload a photo → click Publish.

Within 60 seconds the new car shows up live on the site.

To remove a sold car: click it in the list, hit the delete button, publish. Same thing — gone in a minute.

To change a price or feature on an existing car: click it, change the field, publish.

The admin works on your phone too, so if you snap a photo of a car you just got in, you can post it from the lot.
