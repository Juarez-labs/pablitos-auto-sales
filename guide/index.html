Chapter 1

Logging in to your dashboard

Your website has a private back office where you control everything customers see. You get to it by adding /admin/ to the end of your website address.

  1. Open your admin page

    In any web browser (phone or computer), go to:

    pablitos-auto-sales.netlify.app/admin/

    Once your custom domain finishes connecting, this becomes pablitosautosales.com/admin/ — same login, prettier address. We’ll tell you when it’s ready.

  2. Log in

    Click “Login with Netlify Identity”, then enter the email and password you set up when you accepted your invitation.

  3. You’re in

    You’ll land in your content editor. This is home base for everything in this guide.

Bookmark it Save the admin page to your phone’s home screen or browser favorites so you can get to it in one tap whenever a new car rolls in.
Chapter 2

What you’re looking at

After you log in, you’ll see a simple editor. On the left is a section called 🚗 Inventory. That’s the only thing you need.

  1. Click “Inventory”

    Then click “Cars on the Lot”.

  2. You’ll see your car list

    Every car currently on your website shows up here as a row. Click any row to open it and see its details.

The golden rule Nothing you do here goes live until you click the green “Publish” button in the top-right corner. Until you publish, you’re just working on a draft — so feel free to poke around.
Chapter 3

Adding a car

This is the one you’ll do most. Start to finish, it takes about 90 seconds.

  1. Open your car list

    Inventory → Cars on the Lot.

  2. Click “Add Cars”

    Look for the button to add a new item to the list. A blank form opens up.

  3. Fill in the details

    Go field by field. Here’s exactly what each one means:

FieldWhat to put
YearThe model year. Example: 2012
MakePick from the dropdown: Honda, Toyota, Nissan, or Other.
Model & TrimExample: Camry LE, Civic EX, Altima 2.5 S
MileageJust the number, no commas. Example: 145000
PriceJust the dollar number, no commas or dollar sign. Example: 3800
Price noteOptional. Example: cash or or $200/mo. Leave as “cash” if unsure.
TransmissionAutomatic or Manual.
EngineExample: 4-cyl, V6, or 7 seats for a van.
PhotoClick to upload a picture from your phone or computer. (See Chapter 7 for photo tips.)
Feature tagsShort badges shown on the car. Keep them 2–3 words: Clean Title, Cold A/C, Backup Camera. “Clean Title” gets a green badge automatically.
Top-left tagThe badge on the corner of the photo. Example: Available, Best Value, Just In.
Tag style“Standard” for a navy badge, “Deal” for a bright yellow one that pops.

  1. Click “Publish”

    Green button, top-right. Choose “Publish now” if it asks.

  2. Check your work

    Wait about a minute, then open your website and refresh. Your new car is live for the whole world to see.

Give it a minute After you publish, your website takes about 60 seconds to update. If you don’t see the change right away, wait a moment and refresh the page. That’s normal.
Chapter 4

Changing a price or detail

Dropped the price? Fixed a typo? Same easy flow.

  1. Open the car

    Inventory → Cars on the Lot → click the car you want to change.

  2. Change the field

    Update the price, mileage, features — whatever needs fixing.

  3. Publish

    Green button, top-right. Wait a minute, refresh your site, done.

Chapter 5

Marking a car sold (or removing it)

When a car sells, you’ve got two choices. Both are fine — it’s your preference.

Option A — Show it as SOLD (proves you move cars):

  1. Open the car

    Click it in your list.

  2. Change the Top-left tag

    Type Sold in the “Top-left tag” field.

  3. Change the Tag style to “Deal”

    This makes the SOLD badge bright yellow so it stands out.

  4. Publish

    Done.

Option B — Remove it completely:

  1. Open the car

    Click it in your list.

  2. Delete it

    Look for the trash / delete button on that car’s entry.

  3. Publish

    The car disappears from your website in about a minute.

Deleted the wrong one? Don’t worry — nothing is ever truly lost. Text Jose and it can be brought right back.
Chapter 6

Swapping or adding photos

Got a better shot of a car? Replace it anytime.

  1. Open the car

    Click it in your list.

  2. Find the Photo field

    Click the current photo, then choose “Replace” or the upload button.

  3. Upload the new photo

    Pick it from your phone or computer. It uploads right there.

  4. Publish

    New photo goes live in about a minute.

Chapter 7

Photo tips that sell cars

You don’t need a fancy camera — your phone is perfect. A few habits make your cars look their best:

  1. Shoot in daylight

    A cloudy day is actually ideal — no harsh shadows or glare on the paint.

  2. Three-quarter front angle

    Stand at the front corner of the car, not straight on. Turn the wheels slightly toward you. This is the classic “hero” shot.

  3. Get the whole car in frame

    Don’t cut off the roof or wheels. Leave a little room around the edges.

  4. Clean background helps

    Pull the car to an open part of the lot if you can. Less clutter = more professional.

  5. Skip the filters

    Your website already has its own polished look. Just upload the natural photo.

Chapter 8

  2. Open your admin page

    Tap your bookmark to /admin/ and log in.

  3. Add the car

    Same steps as Chapter 3 — the photo upload pulls straight from your camera roll.

  4. Publish

    The car is on your website before you’ve walked back inside.

Chapter 9

Common questions

I published a typo. How do I fix it?
Open the car again, fix the field, hit Publish. Takes 30 seconds. You can edit as many times as you want.
Why doesn’t my change show up right away?
Your website rebuilds each time you publish. Give it about 60 seconds, then refresh the page. This is normal and keeps the site fast for customers.
Can someone else help me update it?
Yes. We can add up to 5 people with their own logins — a family member or employee. Just tell Jose their email and he’ll send an invite.
I forgot my password.
On the login screen, click “Forgot password” and follow the email. Or text Jose and he’ll send a fresh invite.
Will I break something?
No. You can only edit the car list — the rest of the site is locked. And nothing is permanent: every version is saved, so anything can be undone.
How many cars can I put up?
As many as you want. Add them the same way each time.
